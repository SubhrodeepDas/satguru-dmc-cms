About Satguru

Our Legacy & Global Reach

Founded in 1989 in Kigali, Rwanda, Satguru Travel Group has evolved from a specialized firm into a dominant global leader in travel management, our organization leverages over 30 years of excellence to provide cost-effective, realistic solutions for leisure clients. With a strategic presence in more than 80 countries, we offer complete visibility and peace of mind to travellers.
