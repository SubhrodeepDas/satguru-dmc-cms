Moscow

Russia's dynamic capital blends Soviet grandeur with modern energy — from the iconic Red Square and the Kremlin to world-class restaurants, galleries, and a thriving arts scene.

Russia's cultural heart dazzles with baroque palaces, the world-renowned Hermitage Museum, romantic canals, and the legendary White Nights.

A circle of ancient Russian towns — Sergiev Posad, Vladimir, Suzdal and Yaroslavl — preserving medieval kremlin walls and Orthodox heritage.
